The day I almost dropped out and what changed my mind

AK

Aryan K.

Mar 18, 2026 · 5 min read

✍️

It was week seven of semester two. I hadn't slept properly in days, my assignments were piling up, and I seriously considered calling it all off.

Nobody around me seemed to be struggling the same way. Everyone looked like they had it figured out — submitting on time, attending every lecture, smiling in the corridors.

What changed everything was one conversation with a senior I barely knew. He said something I'll never forget: "Everyone is struggling. Most people are just better at hiding it."
